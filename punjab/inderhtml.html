PUNJAB THE LAND OF COLORS,ENERGY
Punjab,located in the north west of india,is one of the smallest and the most prosperous states of india. The five river Sutlej, Beas, Ravi Chenab and Jhelum gave it its name 'punj-ab' or the 'land of five waters'.These five rivers divide the state into three refions:Majha, Doaba and Malwa.Punjab is primarily an agricultural state and enjoys the natural benefits of fertile soils and abundant wates.As a civilization,it is one of the most ancient in the world with a distinguished culture.
